UNION BUDGET 2025 - KEY TAX CHANGES FOR SALARIED INDIVIDUALS
Presented: February 1, 2025

MAJOR ANNOUNCEMENT: ZERO TAX UP TO ₹12 LAKH INCOME (NEW REGIME)
This is the headline change of Budget 2025.

NEW TAX SLABS (New Regime - Effective FY 2025-26 / AY 2026-27):
Income Range            | Tax Rate
Up to ₹4,00,000         | 0%
₹4,00,001 - ₹8,00,000   | 5%
₹8,00,001 - ₹12,00,000  | 10%
₹12,00,001 - ₹16,00,000 | 15%
₹16,00,001 - ₹20,00,000 | 20%
₹20,00,001 - ₹24,00,000 | 25%
Above ₹24,00,000         | 30%

SECTION 87A REBATE ENHANCED:
- Rebate increased to ₹60,000
- Applicable if net income ≤ ₹12,00,000
- With ₹75,000 standard deduction: zero tax up to ₹12.75L for salaried

NOTE: Above slabs are effective FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27).
For FY 2024-25 (current), the previous slabs apply (₹3L/₹6L/₹9L/₹12L/₹15L).

CHANGES FOR FY 2024-25 (What applies NOW):
No major changes. The following remain:
- Standard Deduction: ₹75,000 (New Regime)
- Standard Deduction: ₹50,000 (Old Regime)
- New Regime remains default
- All deductions same as before

TDS RULES UPDATED:
- TDS threshold on rent enhanced
- New TDS return filing requirements
- Easier TDS credit for salaried employees

UPDATED CAPITAL GAINS TAX:
- STCG (Short Term Capital Gains) on equity: 20% (up from 15%)
- LTCG (Long Term Capital Gains) on equity: 12.5% (up from 10%)
- LTCG exemption threshold: ₹1.25L (up from ₹1L)
- Debt mutual funds: Slab rate taxation continues

NPS CHANGES:
- NPS Vatsalya introduced for minors
- Contribution on behalf of minor child qualifies under 80C

IMPACT ON TAX PLANNING FOR FY 2024-25:
1. Old vs New Regime breakeven analysis still critical
2. Capital gains strategy: Book LTCG up to ₹1.25L annually (tax-free)
3. NPS still excellent for additional ₹50,000 deduction (Old Regime)
4. Health insurance becomes more important with medical inflation

PLANNING FOR FY 2025-26 (Advance):
- New regime becomes significantly more attractive
- Zero tax for income up to ₹12.75L with standard deduction
- Old Regime only beneficial if deductions > ₹5L+ approximately
