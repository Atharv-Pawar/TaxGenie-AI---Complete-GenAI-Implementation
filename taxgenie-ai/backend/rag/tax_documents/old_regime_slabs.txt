OLD TAX REGIME - FY 2024-25 (AY 2025-26)
Income Tax Act, 1961 (Pre-amended slabs)

NOTE: Old Regime must be ACTIVELY OPTED for from FY 2023-24 onwards.

TAX SLABS (Old Regime FY 2024-25):
Income Range            | Tax Rate
Up to ₹2,50,000         | 0%
₹2,50,001 - ₹5,00,000   | 5%
₹5,00,001 - ₹10,00,000  | 20%
Above ₹10,00,000         | 30%

SECTION 87A REBATE (Old Regime):
- Rebate up to ₹12,500
- Applicable if net taxable income ≤ ₹5,00,000
- Makes effective tax ZERO for income up to ₹5L

SURCHARGE (Old Regime):
Income ₹50L - ₹1Cr: 10% surcharge
Income ₹1Cr - ₹2Cr: 15% surcharge
Income ₹2Cr - ₹5Cr: 25% surcharge
Income > ₹5Cr: 37% surcharge

HEALTH AND EDUCATION CESS: 4% on (tax + surcharge)

STANDARD DEDUCTION: ₹50,000

KEY DEDUCTIONS AVAILABLE:
Section 80C       : ₹1,50,000 (ELSS, PPF, PF, LIC, etc.)
Section 80D       : ₹25,000 self + ₹25,000 parents (₹50,000 seniors)
Section 80CCD(1B) : ₹50,000 (NPS, OVER 80C limit)
Section 80E       : Full interest on education loan
Section 80G       : 50-100% of eligible donations
Section 80TTA     : ₹10,000 on savings account interest
Section 24(b)     : ₹2,00,000 on home loan interest (self-occupied)
HRA Exemption     : As per 10(13A) formula
LTA Exemption     : Actual travel cost, 2 journeys in 4-year block

EXAMPLE TAX CALCULATION (Old Regime):
Gross Salary: ₹12,00,000
Less: Standard Deduction: ₹50,000
Less: Section 80C: ₹1,50,000 (PF + LIC + ELSS)
Less: Section 80D: ₹25,000 (health insurance)
Less: NPS 80CCD(1B): ₹50,000
Less: Professional Tax: ₹2,400
Taxable Income: ₹12,00,000 - ₹2,77,400 = ₹9,22,600

Tax:
0-2.5L: ₹0
2.5-5L: ₹12,500 (5%)
5-9.22L: ₹84,520 (20%)
Total Tax: ₹97,020
+ 4% Cess: ₹3,881
Total: ₹1,00,901

WHO BENEFITS FROM OLD REGIME:
- Those who can claim deductions > ₹3.75L-4.25L
- Home loan borrowers (principal + interest combined)
- High HRA earners in metro cities
- Investors with full 80C utilisation

SENIOR CITIZENS (Old Regime - Special Slabs):
Age 60-79 (Senior Citizen):
Up to ₹3,00,000: 0%
₹3L-5L: 5%
₹5L-10L: 20%
Above ₹10L: 30%

Age 80+ (Super Senior Citizen):
Up to ₹5,00,000: 0%
₹5L-10L: 20%
Above ₹10L: 30%
