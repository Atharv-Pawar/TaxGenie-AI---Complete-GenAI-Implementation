SECTION 80G - DEDUCTION FOR DONATIONS TO CHARITABLE INSTITUTIONS
FY 2024-25 | Income Tax Act, 1961

AVAILABLE UNDER: Old Tax Regime ONLY

DEDUCTION CATEGORIES:

100% DEDUCTION (No qualifying limit):
- PM National Relief Fund
- National Defence Fund
- PM Cares Fund
- National Foundation for Communal Harmony
- Approved universities/educational institutions
- National Blood Transfusion Council
- Zila Saksharta Samiti

50% DEDUCTION (No qualifying limit):
- PM Drought Relief Fund
- National Children's Fund
- Jawaharlal Nehru Memorial Fund
- Indira Gandhi Memorial Trust
- Rajiv Gandhi Foundation

100% DEDUCTION (Subject to 10% qualifying limit):
- Donations to government for family planning
- Donations to Indian Olympic Association
- Approved sports funds

50% DEDUCTION (Subject to 10% qualifying limit):
- Any other approved charitable institution
- Religious trusts
- Municipal corporations or government for development

QUALIFYING LIMIT (for "subject to" categories):
Maximum = 10% of Adjusted Gross Total Income
Adjusted GTI = GTI minus long-term capital gains, short-term capital gains,
               and deductions under 80C to 80U (except 80G)

PAYMENT MODE:
- Cash donations: Only up to ₹2,000 qualify
- Above ₹2,000: Must use bank transfer, cheque, DD, online payment
- No deduction for donations in kind (clothes, food, etc.)

DOCUMENTATION:
- Receipt from the institution showing their 80G registration number
- Form 10BE issued by the institution (for donations ≥ ₹2,000)
- PAN of the institution required

EXAMPLE:
Donation to PM Cares Fund: ₹10,000
100% deduction, no qualifying limit
Deduction: ₹10,000
Tax saving at 20%: ₹2,000
Tax saving at 30%: ₹3,000

HOW TO VERIFY 80G STATUS:
Check at: incometaxindia.gov.in → Search for Exemptions
Look for Form CSR-1 for CSR donations
